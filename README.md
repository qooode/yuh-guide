# Apple TV Audio & Video Setup

A step-by-step guide to configuring every audio and video setting on your Apple TV 4K for the best possible playback experience.

Set it once, never think about it again.

---

### What this covers

**Video** — Format, HDMI output, chroma, match dynamic range, match frame rate

**Audio** — Audio format (LPCM), audio mode, reduce loud sounds, volume fixes

**HDMI chain** — TV black level, signal format, pulldown/telecine

**Dolby Vision** — Profile 5, 8, and 7 explained

**Fusion player** — Cache settings and unsupported codec list

---

### Why this exists

Getting the best picture and sound out of an Apple TV means changing settings across the Apple TV itself, your TV, and sometimes your soundbar or receiver. Most guides skip half the chain or don't tell you where to actually find each setting. This one walks through everything with exact navigation paths.
